The Department of Mechanical Engineering at GL BAJAJ Institute of Technology & Management, Greater Noida was established in 2005. The department offer 4 year B. Tech. Program in Mechanical Engineering. The department provides a strong foundation for the overall growth and development of the students by enriching them with technical, analytical, quantitative, reasoning, ethical & linguistic qualities. The department has a pool of well qualified and experienced faculty having backgrounds from IIT, NIT and other nationally and internationally recognized institutes in various research areas including Computational Fluid Dynamics, FEM, Rapid prototyping, Smart materials, Combustion etc.
