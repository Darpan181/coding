The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management was established in the year 2005 with a vision to help the IT boom and fulfil the need of dynamic Software Engineers globally. Since then, especially in the last ten years of the IT revolution, the department has taken long strides and is now among one of the best department in the college. The Department aims to nurture students in terms of modern computer techniques and to prepare them, to cope well with the technical advancements in future. Computer Science being the flagship branch of Engineering takes focus in scientific research, scientific programming, and software engineering. The department is the proud record holder of University results as well as placements in industries. The Department also holds the record of best placement percentage in the college till date. Several fortune 500 IT industries such as Amazon, TCS, Accenture, Infosys, Wipro, Persistent Systems, Zensar,, L & T Info-tech, KPIT visit our campus for campus Interviews and recruitments and end up with good amount of student signing up for these groups.
